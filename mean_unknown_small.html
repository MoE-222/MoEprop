Confidence Interval for Mean

t-Student | Small Sample (n < 30)

Conditions for using this formula

  • Population approximately normal
  • Sample size n < 30 (small sample)
  • Population variance σ² unknown
  • Use t-Student distribution instead of Z
  • Degrees of freedom = n - 1

General Formula:

X̄ ± t(α/2, n-1) × S/√n
Degrees of freedom (df) = n - 1

Application Example

Sample of 12 cotton yarn strength measurements gave mean 7.38 and std dev 1.24. 95% confidence interval (t0.025,11 = 2.201):

7.38 ± 2.201 × 1.24/√12 = 7.38 ± 0.787
6.51 < μ < 8.25
