Confidence Interval for Mean

Unknown Variance - Large Sample (n ≥ 30)

Conditions for using this formula

  • Sample size n ≥ 30 (large sample)
  • Population variance σ² unknown
  • Use sample standard deviation S
  • Use Z distribution (due to large sample size)

General Formula:

X̄ ± Zα/2 × S/√n
Key difference: Use S (sample) instead of σ (population)

Application Example

Random sample of 36 university students gave quality points mean 2.6 and std dev 0.3. 95% confidence interval:

2.6 ± 1.96 × 0.3/6 = 2.6 ± 0.098
2.50 < μ < 2.70
